world-cup-tracker

After a week of studying html and css, I had a week's break, from my bootcamp course. I wanted to build something so I could practice what I had learned, and everyone was talking about the World Cup, so I made this, for my friends and family to track the world cup. I made some divs for each date, and inside them I put more divs for each game in a grid format. I researched how to make it responsive, (using media queries), so friends could keep track on a mobile. I found out how to add a flag for each country (using a cdn), and after each game, is a link to a short highlights video. I do have to keep updating the html though, after every game, which hopefullyy in the future, I'll be able to fix. I might make another one for the next world cup (2026) and compare my skills.
